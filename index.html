About High Definition

We are First Tech Challenge (FTC) Team 18225 High Definition, a robotics team from Seattle, Washington composed of 14 enthusiastic and motivated students from the 7th-12th grades. In 2020-2021, our rookie season, we won the Inspire award at the FTC Washington State Championships and placed 5th in the robot game. We were also 1 of 20 FTC Global Innovation Challenge Finalists for the development of Heart-and-Sole, an innovative fitness app.
